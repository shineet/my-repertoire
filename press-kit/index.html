About Shine

Most corporate entertainment is watched. Mentalism is experienced. Shine turns your own guests into the stars of the impossible — thoughts revealed, decisions predicted, and moments your team is still talking about at the next quarterly meeting.

Before he took the stage, Shine spent 20+ years as a technology executive. That background is his edge: he knows exactly what a corporate room needs — entertainment that is smart, polished, and completely safe in front of your executives, your clients, and your HR team. No embarrassment, no crude material, no awkward moments. Just genuine astonishment.

Based in Austin and performing across Central Texas and beyond, Shine tailors every performance to the event in front of him — weaving in your company name, your product, or your event theme so the show feels made for your room, because it is.

Section 05

What Shine Needs From Your Venue

  • Strolling sets: nothing at all — no setup, no stage, no A/V.
  • Stage shows: a sound system with a wireless microphone and a small performance area.
  • Larger rooms (150+): a raised stage and a screen/projector are helpful but not required.
  • Timing: Shine coordinates the run-of-show directly with you or your venue/planner.
  • Paperwork: performance agreement, W-9, and certificate of insurance available on request.
